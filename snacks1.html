Making is also quite straightforward. Here's a simple recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Assorted fruits of your choice (e.g., pineapple, watermelon, banana, orange, mango, grapes, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon of honey (optional) - 1 tablespoon of lemon juice (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

    • How to Make:

  • Wash and peel the fruits, depending on their type.
  • Cut the fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  • In a large bowl, combine the fruit pieces.
  • If desired, drizzle with honey and lemon juice, and toss gently.
  • Serve chilled, garnished with fresh mint leaves or a sprinkle of sugar, if desired.

    • Some fruits used include:

  • Pineapple
  • Watermelon
  • Mango
  • Orange
  • Banana
  • Guava
  • Pawpaw (papaya)

    • Tips:

  • Choose seasonal fruits for better flavor and affordability.
  • You can customize the fruit salad to your taste preferences.
  • Consider adding a splash of citrus juice to prevent browning.
  • Enjoy your delicious fruit salad!
