Here's a simple recipe to cook white rice and stew:

White Rice:

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Instructions for White Rice:
  • Rinse the rice: Rinse the rice in a fine mesh sieve until the water runs clear.
  • Cook the rice: In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the water is absorbed.
  • Season with salt: Fluff the cooked rice with a fork and add salt to taste.

    • Stew:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • 1 cup beef or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Protein of your choice (e.g., beef, chicken, or fish.)

    • How to cook Stew:

  • Heat oil: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  • Saut√© onions: Add chopped onions and cook until translucent.
  • Add garlic and ginger: Add minced garlic and grated ginger and cook for 1 minute.
  • Add protein: Add your chosen protein and cook until browned.
  • Add tomatoes and broth: Add chopped tomatoes and broth. Stir well.
  • Season: Add curry powder (if using), salt, and pepper to taste.
  • Simmer: Simmer the stew for 20-30 minutes or until the flavors have melded together and the protein is cooked through.

    • Serve:

  • Serve the stew over the cooked white rice.

    • Tips:

  • Use a ratio of 2:1 water to rice for perfect cooked rice.
  • Adjust the stew's spice level to your taste.
  • You can customize the stew with your favorite ingredients and spices.

    • Enjoy your delicious white rice and stew!
