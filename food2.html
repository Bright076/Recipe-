Here's a simple recipe to cook delicious jollof rice and chicken:

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the jollof rice:

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2-3 Scotch bonnet peppers or habanero peppers (optional, for some heat)

    • How to cook:

  • 1.Marinate the chicken: Mix chicken pieces with curry powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  • 2.Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  • 3.Prepare the jollof rice: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  • 4.Add garlic and ginger: Add minced garlic and grated ginger and cook for 1 minute.
  • 5.Add tomato and spices: Add diced tomato, curry powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • 6.Add rice and water: Add uncooked rice and water to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  • 7.Add cooked chicken: Add cooked chicken to the pot and stir gently.
  • 8.Cook the jollof rice: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and fluffy.
  • 9.Serve: Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh herbs or scallions.

    • Tips:

  • Use long-grain rice for the best results.
  • Adjust the amount of Scotch bonnet peppers or habanero peppers to your desired level of spiciness.
  • You can also add other ingredients like bell peppers, carrots, or peas to the jollof rice.

    • Enjoy your delicious jollof rice and chicken!
