Here's a simple recipe for Fried Rice and Chicken:

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 1 pound boneless, make the chicken breast or thighs skinless, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the fried rice:

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper for taste

    • How to cook

  • 1.To Cook the chicken:* Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and garlic powder. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • 2. To Prepare the fried rice: Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add diced onion and cook until translucent. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  • 3. Add cooked rice: Add the cooked rice to the skillet or wok and stir-fry for about 5 minutes, breaking up any clumps.
  • 4. Add beaten eggs: Push the rice mixture to one side of the pan or pot. Add a little more oil if needed, then pour in beaten eggs. Scramble the eggs until cooked through, then mix with the rice mixture.
  • 5. Adding the chicken to the fried rice: Add cooked chicken to the fried rice mixture and stir-fry for another one minute. Add soy sauce to taste.
  • 6. Serve: Transfer the fried rice and chicken to a serving platter.

    • Tips:

  • Use day-old rice to make the best fried rice.
  • Customize the recipe with your favorite vegetables or protein sources.
  • Add flavor with soy sauce, oyster sauce, or other seasonings.

    • Enjoy your delicious fried rice and chicken.
